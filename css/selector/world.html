Flowers

Rose

Roses are one of the most loved flowers in the world. These stunning and fragrant symbols of love have been in cultivation for thousands of years. They come in all sorts of fantastical forms and exquisite colours that are enjoyed in ornamental displays.

Lotus

The lotus flower is regarded as the National Flower of India. Lotus symbolises beauty, purity and knowledge. It is an aquatic flower with round leaves floating on the water. It grows in freshwater lakes and shallow ponds.

Sunflower

Sunflowers are useful plants with large flower heads, or blooms. They are named for the way they turn their blooms from east to west to follow the Sun. Sunflowers were first grown in North and South America. Today they are also grown in other parts of the world, including Europe and Asia.

Daliga

Dahlias are perennial plants with a range of features across 42 species. Some have green herby stems, others have woody stems. The flowers are incredibly diverse, with colours including white, yellow, orange, red and purple of varying shades.
